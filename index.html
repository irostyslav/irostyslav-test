Hobbies

Variant Sudoku

Variant sudoku puzzles follow standard sudoku rules but add a twist — sometimes you color cells that share a value without knowing it, and sometimes you construct the regions yourself. I have beginner recommendations if you want to dive in.

Hanabi

A cooperative card game where you see everyone's hand except your own. Players exchange color and number clues to help teammates play cards in order. Simple rules, surprisingly deep strategy.
